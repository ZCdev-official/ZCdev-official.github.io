"Good design is as little design as possible."

Dieter Rams

How should we think about code?

The most important part about code design is simplicity. Anyone can make code that is complex, but complicated code just confuses everyone. As a result, the goal of code should not be finding a solution to a problem. The goal should be finding a simple way to solve a very complex problem. This philosophy is what seperates regular coders from excellent coders.

How should we approach design?

Just like code, design should be very simple. However, it is much more important to make sure that your design makes sense. If something is not necessary, it should be removed. In addition, your design needs to display your code in an understandable way. It should make your project easier to understand by translating your code for the user.

Why do I need to publish my work?

It is absolutely essential to publish your work. When you publish your work, you are allowing anyone to appreciate what you have done. If your project is good, or even bad, you will get excellent feedback from other people. In addition, you may even get introduced to new opportunities because you published your work. Ultimately, the main reason to publish is so your targeted audience can enjoy what you have created.

What are the benefits of combining code and design?

Focusing on only code or only design works, but it misses an amazing opportunity. When you combine the aspects of code and design, your final product will turn out fantastic. When working on both, your code will correspond with a part of your design. This ultimately makes your whole project flow in a way that was not possible before.
